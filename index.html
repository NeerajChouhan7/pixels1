The Problem

Two worlds that don't speak.

Technology understands data. Media understands emotion. Pixels & Pen is the bridge — a creative incubator that makes both fluent in the other's language.

Tech Knows Data

Distribution, platforms, audience analytics, performance metrics — but not how to make films that feel alive. They have the reach. They lack the soul.

We bridge

Media Knows Craft

Emotion, story, visual language, human truth — but not the resources, networks, or platforms to scale. They have the vision. They lack the infrastructure.

The Model

Three IPs. One ecosystem.

Each program feeds the next. Together they form a self-sustaining creative engine — for filmmakers, artists, and the industry watching closely.

01

Filmmaker Program

Ten filmmakers selected from across India. Each receives ₹10 lakh to produce one short film. The brief is simple: fail, learn, grow, repeat. In return — money to make a film, India's best collaborators, a producer credit, and a direct path to pitching a feature.

₹10L per filmmaker
02

The Podcast

Deep, unscripted conversations on the craft of advertising and cinema. Modelled on clip-first growth — YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels — to build a real audience of working creatives. Guests include directors, editors, and brand storytellers who've shaped modern Indian visual culture.

Clip-first distribution
03

PVR Screening

All ten short films. One curated event. A real cinema screen. Live masterclasses with industry leaders, networking with directors and DPs, and an audience that remembers your name. This is their MAMI — intimate, earned, and built on trust.

Theatrical event

Partnership Tiers

This isn't charity.

Three sponsor categories. Each gets something specific, valuable, and available nowhere else in India right now.

Tech Companies

They want media talent.

AI tools, SaaS platforms, and tech brands need creative pipelines. We give them the most promising filmmakers in India — before anyone else has seen them.

Production Houses

They want their next director.

We surface, train, and credential the talent they've been searching for. First access to the next wave — before the market prices them in.

Tooling Companies

They want creator credibility.

Camera brands, editing software, grip and lighting. Being the gear of choice for the next generation of Indian filmmakers is invaluable. We make that happen.
